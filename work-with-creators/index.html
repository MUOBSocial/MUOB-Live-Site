Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of creators do you work with?

We work with vetted travel creators specialising in hospitality content. Our network includes photographers, videographers, bloggers, and influencers who create high-quality content for hotels. All creators are screened for engagement rates and content quality.

Do I get to approve the creator?

Yes, we'll send you creator profiles to review before finalizing any collaboration. You have full control over who visits your property. We'll only proceed with creators you approve.

Is this free?

Yes — you're offering value in kind (accommodation or experiences) rather than cash payments. There are no fees to use our matching service.

Can I use the content on my own channels?

Yes, most creators are happy for you to repost the content on your hotel’s social media. If you’d like full commercial usage (e.g. for ads or your website), we’ll make sure that’s agreed with the creator in advance.

How long does the process take?

Typically 2-4 weeks from initial brief to creator visit, depending on your location and requirements. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.
