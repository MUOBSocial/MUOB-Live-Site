Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our social media service

Do I need professional camera equipment?

Not at all! A modern smartphone is all you need. Our filming guides are specifically designed for smartphone recording, and we provide detailed instructions on how to get the best quality footage. We can also advise on simple, affordable accessories that can enhance your videos if desired.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients begin seeing meaningful engagement increases within the first 30 days. For booking increases, the typical timeline is 60-90 days, though this can vary based on your location, seasonality, and starting point. We provide detailed monthly reports so you can track progress clearly.

What if I don't have time to film every month?

We understand hotel operations are demanding. Many clients choose to batch-film several months of content in a single day when occupancy is lower. We can help you create a filming schedule that works with your operational rhythms. We also offer on-site filming services for an additional fee if you prefer to completely outsource this step.

Do you handle responses and comments?

Our Standard and Full-Time packages include basic comment monitoring and flagging important interactions for your attention. For full community management (responding to all comments and messages), we offer this as an add-on service. Many clients find that once they have a consistent posting schedule, engaging with comments becomes much more manageable in-house.

Can I cancel if I'm not seeing results?

Absolutely. We operate on month-to-month contracts with no long-term commitment required. We're confident in our ability to deliver results, which is why we don't lock clients into lengthy contracts. If you're not seeing the value, you can cancel anytime with 14 days' notice.

What platforms do you publish content on?

We create and publish content on TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, and YouTube Shorts. These platforms currently offer the highest engagement and reach for hospitality businesses. If there are specific platforms you'd like to focus on more heavily, we can customize your strategy accordingly.
